Dataset Overview

The predictions on this platform are powered by the Bengaluru House Price dataset. After extensive cleaning and preprocessing to remove outliers and handle missing values, the dataset consists of high-quality records representing the real estate market.

Key Features: Location, Built-up Area, Bedrooms (BHK), Bathrooms, Gym, Pool, Garden
Target Variable: Price (in Lakhs)
Dimensionality Reduction: Locations with fewer than 10 data points were grouped into "Other" to prevent model overfitting.
Amenities Added: has_gym, has_swimming_pool, has_ground, ground_area_sqft, amenities_count
Final Dataset: 13,225 clean rows · 15 ML features · 80/20 train-test split
